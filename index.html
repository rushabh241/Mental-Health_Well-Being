Mental Health And Well Being --->



Mental health and well-being are comprehensive states that encompass an individual's emotional, psychological, and social health. These intertwined concepts reflect one's ability to navigate life's challenges, cultivate positive relationships, and maintain a sense of equilibrium and satisfaction.



Mental health encompasses emotional balance, which means understanding, expressing, and managing emotions in constructive ways. It involves psychological resilience, including traits like self-confidence and self-awareness.



Well-being, on the other hand, encompasses a broader spectrum. It includes physical health, involving regular exercise, balanced nutrition, adequate sleep, and overall vitality. Emotional well-being is another dimension, entailing the ability to experience a full range of emotions while having the skills to manage and regulate them constructively.
